As our work, our food, our homes and our transportation become increasingly governed by algorithms, their behaviours also become more opaque, unpredictible and complex.

Users are bound to have hard time keeping up with the speed and magnitude of these kinds of movements. What we need is new tools to extend our abilities to see, interact with and act on these systems.My goal is to design products that exists at the center of consumers’ life and adapt to users’ needs and behavior.

Other than design I spend considerable time reading, and have read numerous classic titles from Albert Camus to Jack Kerouac. Other than fiction, classic and design books I am also intersted in books with alternative narrative.
